
event `Senza Solaria Stocks Asteroid Young 4`
	shipyard "Senza Solaria Ships"
		`Asteroid Young 4`

mission `Senza Solaria Supply Asteroid Young 4`
	invisible
	landing
	source
		planet "Senza Solaria"
	to offer
		and	
			has `ship model: Asteroid Young 4`
			not `flagship model: Asteroid Young 4`
	on offer
		conversation
			scene "ship/asteroid young 4"
			`Upon arriving at Senza Solaria With your Asteroid Young 4, before you can even decide where to go, a group of researchers are already there to meet you the moment you step off of your ship.`
			`	"Our scanners detected a ship unknown to them within your fleet, would you be willing to donate it for us to research it?" says a researcher.`
			choice
				`	(Donate the Asteroid Young 4 to be researched.) WARNING: ALL INSTALLED OUTFITS WILL BE LOST.`
					goto donate
				`	(Don't donate.)`
					defer
			label donate
			`"Ah, perfect! We appreciate your donation, and assure you, your contribution will not go to waste!"`
			`The engineers guide your ship to an isolated landing pad a few miles from the spaceport, and carefully transport it inside of one of their research facilities."`
			``
			`	After idling at the spaceport for a few hours, you hear rumors of a new ship entering production soon at Senza Solaria.`
				accept
	on accept
		take ship `Asteroid Young 4`
			"count" 1
		event `Senza Solaria Stocks Asteroid Young 4`
