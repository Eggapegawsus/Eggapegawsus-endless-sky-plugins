
event `Senza Solaria Stocks Subsidurial`
	shipyard "Senza Solaria Ships"
		`Subsidurial`

mission `Senza Solaria Supply Subsidurial`
	invisible
	landing
	source
		planet "Senza Solaria"
	to offer
		and	
			has `ship model: Subsidurial`
			not `flagship model: Subsidurial`
	on offer
		conversation
			scene "thumbnail/subsidurial"
			`Upon arriving at Senza Solaria With your Subsidurial, before you can even decide where to go, a group of researchers are already there to meet you the moment you step off of your ship.`
			`	"Ah... one of these, huh? You know, they're kind almost kind of cute... Would you be willing to donate it for us to research it?" says a researcher.`
			choice
				`	(Donate the Subsidurial to be researched.) WARNING: ALL INSTALLED OUTFITS WILL BE LOST.`
					goto donate
				`	(Don't donate.)`
					defer
			label donate
			`"Ah, perfect! We appreciate your donation, and assure you, your contribution will not go to waste!"`
			`The engineers guide the Subsidurial to an isolated landing pad a few miles from the spaceport. From a distance, you can just barely see one of them checking to see if anyone is nearby, before they begin petting and playing with the subsidurial for a breif while.  Afterwards, they carefully transport it inside of one of their research facilities."`
			``
			`	After idling at the spaceport for a few hours, you hear rumors of a new ship entering production soon at Senza Solaria.`
				accept
	on accept
		take ship `Subsidurial`
			"count" 1
		event `Senza Solaria Stocks Subsidurial`
