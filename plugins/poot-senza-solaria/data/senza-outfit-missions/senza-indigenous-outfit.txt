
event "Senza Solaria Stocks Absorption Organ"
	outfitter "Senza Solaria Outfits"
		"Absorption Organ"
mission "Senza Solaria Supply Absorption Organ"
	invisible
	landing
	source
		planet "Senza Solaria"
	to offer
		has "outfit (cargo): Absorption Organ"
	on offer
		conversation
			scene "outfit/unknown"
			`Upon arriving at Senza Solaria, before you can even decide where to go, a group of researchers are already there to meet you the moment you step off of your ship.`
			`	"Our scanners detected an unknown... organic material of sorts, aboard your ship. Well, I suppose... would you be willing to donate it for us to research?" says a researcher.`
			choice
				`	(Donate the Absorption Organ to be researched.)`
					goto donate
				`	(Don't donate.)`
					defer
			label donate
			`"Very well, thank you for this... donation..."`
			`The engineers carefully extract the donation from your ship, and take it to their research facility to research."`
			``
			`	After idling at the spaceport for a few hours, you hear rumors of a new stock arriving soon at Senza Solaria.`
				accept
	on accept
		outfit "Absorption Organ" -1
		event "Senza Solaria Stocks Absorption Organ"
