#       POOT Core ideas
#   Peripherals Or Outstanding Trinkets is a plugin that originally started out as a very small plugin that allowed you to infinitely expand ships to install any outfit. It then evolved over the course of 8 years, having gone through 5 different total re-writes/reworks, eventually evolving into a massive vanilla-plus plugin that is intended to add all the features I wanted when playing endless sky. After making multiple smaller plugins, i decided to add some additional gameplay features/content to expand what you could do in the game (Hence, senza solaria, capturable author ships, capturable everything, system conversions, stronger versions of almost every weapon in the game).

#   The primary goal of POOT as it currently stands is to expand the gameplay as much as absolutely possible, greatly extending what you can do in the game outside of just the story. I LOVE the gameplay of endless sky, but it always felt like there was so little to work for, or to try and do outside of missions, so I wanted to make as many features that could add things to be done, different ways to play the game, more stuff to find or work towards. I also didn't want to totally destroy the balance of the game in the process, but also make it so you could go further beyond what you could do in the base game, which is... difficult to accomplish, to say the least lmao. In the vanilla game, you don't even have to beat the free worlds to get a massive fleet of quarg warships and go conquer the galaxy, but I always wanted there to be more than just building a massive fleet, have more to do, more to find, more to customize or push beyond some sort of limit. Hence, POOT, and all its small or large features that (hopefully) open up quite a few more possibilities for what you can do within a single playthrough.
