# Infinite Expanse
This plugin adds outfits that can be used to expand a ships outfit space or cargo space indefinitely. It also has outfits that can directly add engine and weapon space, but with additional costs. 
<br>

There are two types of outfits: Void Expansions, and Infentessimal Expansions
 - Both are listed under "Void Expansions" in the outfitter, below Licenses
<br>

# Infentessimal Expansions

![Infentessimal Expansions](/images/infinite_expanse_2.png) 

 Infentessimal Expansions are availible for Cargo and Outfit Space. They have no general downsides, other than being somewhat highly priced.

  - Regular versions cost 200,000 Credits each, and expand by 20 Cargo or Outfit Space

  - XL versions cost 10,000,000 Credits each, and expand by 1,000 Cargo or Outfit Space

<br>

# Void Expansions

![Infentessimal Expansions](/images/infinite_expanse_3.png) 

<br>

 Void Expansions are availible for Cargo, Outfit, Weapon, Engine, and a special Mass Reducing expansion. They are a intended to be a more balanced infinite expansion, with higher overall costs, heat generation, and a need for existing space in order to be installed.
  - XL versions are availible, being x1000 to all stats of S versions
